About KemoArt Kreatives

Where creativity meets purpose. We turn ideas into stunning visuals.

Our Story

KemoArt Kreatives was founded by Kipkemoi Amos, a passionate visual artist, designer, and digital storyteller. What started as a dream to blend imagination and design has grown into a creative studio helping individuals and businesses stand out.

With a strong background in photography and graphic design, we serve clients across Kenya with branding, poster design, business cards, animations, and digital content creation. Each project is handled with care, creativity, and a personal touch.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and businesses by delivering creative design solutions that connect, inspire, and stand out.
